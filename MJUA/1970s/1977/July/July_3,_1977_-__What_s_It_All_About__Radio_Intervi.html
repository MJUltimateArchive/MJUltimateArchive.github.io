"What's It All About" Radio Interview

Air date confirmed on the 7” single’s label (archived) (“Broadcast July 1977”)

 

Interview summary, MJJCollectors item listing (archived)

The A side of this red labeled 45 features The Jacksons (Program 380):  "An Interview With The Group, Featuring Their Hit Songs".  Jackie, Tito, Marlon, and Michael are interviewed by host Bill Huie.

Jackie talks about how “everyone has chores to do” and the brothers discuss how they performed for the Mayor of Gary Indiana before being discovered by Bobby Taylor.  Tito talks about how “imitators” like The Osmonds, The Sylvers, and The Franco Family did not offend them, but flattered them.  They talk about recording with Gamble & Huff, and recall how they used to sell pictures of the group for a quarter and were often warned but “don’t get the big head”.
