"Wonderama"

 

Ann Reinking, played Roxie Hart in original “Chicago” musical, “New York Daily News” (March 26, 2000) (archived)

She recalls an appearance she made in a children's television show called Wonderama during her 1970s run in the initial Chicago production. The Jackson Five were on the same show.

"I did 'Me and My Baby' - all these classic struts, leg over leg; it's sexy, sophisticated and fun.

"Michael Jackson came up to me afterward and said, 'Who choreographed that for you?' I said Bob Fosse, and he wrote it down on his hand."
