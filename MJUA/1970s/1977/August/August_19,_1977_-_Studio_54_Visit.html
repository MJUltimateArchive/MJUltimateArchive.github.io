Studio 54 Visit

Date confirmed by Getty Images (Image 001 is dated “August 19, 1977… Photo by Vernon Shibla/New York Post Archives”)

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Michael became a frequent visitor at Studio 54, often accompanied by Liza Minnelli or his brothers, and enjoyed several events there. Epic Records organized a birthday [event] for his 19th birthday. Specially selected guests were given a unique invitation ticket in the shape of an aeroplane in keeping with the theme of The Jacksons new album ’GOIN’ PLACES’.
