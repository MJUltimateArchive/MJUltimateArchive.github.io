"The Wiz" Press Conference

Date confirmed in “Rock & Soul” magazine (“28th September 1977”)

 

“Rock & Soul” magazine (January 1971), From Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (article summary/quotes)

Another part of the Rock & Soul “The Wiz” Report.

At a champagne breakfast on 28th September, 1977 at Astoria Studios, just before The Wiz was about to commence filming, Michael met the press along with Diana Ross.

“I’m really excited because I love the story. My favourite character in the original movie – “The Wizard Of Oz” – was always the scarecrow. I think he’s bright and I love everything about him.”

“I love all the people in the cast, and I’m especially happy to be working with Diana again. We’re having a great time.”
