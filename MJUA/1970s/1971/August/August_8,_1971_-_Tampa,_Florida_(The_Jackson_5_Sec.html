Tampa, Florida (The Jackson 5 Second National Tour)

 

“16” magazine (February 1972), From Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (article summary/quotes)

This issue of 16 Magazine February 1972 carries an article by two girls who followed the Jackson 5 to Miami and New York with their friends. Sandra Holcomb and Sandra Fludd talk about meeting them at their hotel and having photos taken, before the Jackson 5 did their concert.

...Sandra Fludd’s opportunity came before the concert at Miami Beach Auditorium, Miami Beach, Florida on 8th August, 1971. It was very much the same as Sandra Holcomb’s meeting, only she and her friends returned the next day to see their heroes at the hotel.
