"The Brady Bunch Visits ABC"

Air date confirmed on photo release envelope (archived) (“Friday, September 10”)

 

“16” magazine (May 1972), from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

The Jackson 5 made an appearance on The Brady Bunch Visits ABC” Special 10th September 1971. It was a mini appearance by the J5 as they were filming their Goin’ Back To Indiana special and promoting their cartoon series.

There was a story in 16 magazine in May 1972 of Michael’s meeting with the Brady’s in the article “The Secret Life Of Michael Jackson”.

In the short meeting he and his brothers had with the Brady’s Michael swapped telephone numbers with Chris Knight and Maureen McCormick. It was a few months before they all connected via telephone, but they discovered they had plenty of things in common. Maureen was just recording her first record and asked Michael for some tips. Michael enquired about something he was interested in which was acting. Michael contacted Knight after spending a couple of hours talking to Maureen McCormick. The trio agreed to meet up at the skating rink on 8th January, 1973. Unfortunately Knight had to cry off with flu. So Michael sent him a get well card.

Michael took Marlon and Randy along as his chaperones to the ice rink. There they met Maureen McCormick, Susan Olsen and Eve Plumb in what proved to be an enjoyable day. Eve’s father took pictures of them all on the rink.

Maureen McCormick spoke about their day at the Topanga Plaza skating rink in her memoirs “Here’s the Story: Surviving Marcia Brady And Finding My True Voice”

“Once we went ice-skating and he held my hand as we glided around the rink.

I wondered if he might try to kiss me, but he didn’t. After another outing, he did give me a kiss goodbye. But it was only a gentle peck on the cheek.”

“Retroality” (July 2009), From Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

In an interview with Retroality. TV in July 2009 Susan Olsen, who played Cindy Brady, said that she was there that day at the Topanga Plaza skating rink.

“Yeah. I would have one outing with him. Outing? We went ice skating. Michael and Marlon and Randy. And Maureen and Eve and me.

Yeah, she says it was an intimate date between she and Michael. She had a crush on Jermaine!”
