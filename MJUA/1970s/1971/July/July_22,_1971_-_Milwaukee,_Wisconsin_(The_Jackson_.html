Milwaukee, Wisconsin (The Jackson 5 Second National Tour)

Venue and date confirmed in July 17, 1971 “Billboard” magazine (“...In its Miller High Life Jazz Oasis… The Jackson 5 will make a Summerfest ‘71 concert appearance on July 22”)

 

“Right On! Focus” magazine (1984)

...In 1971 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, The Jackson Five drew an unprecedented 115,000 people to one of their concerts. To escape the throng of hysterical girls, the Jackson boys were forced to be taken away in a police boat back to their hotel.
