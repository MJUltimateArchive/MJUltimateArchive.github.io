New York City, New York (The Jackson 5 Second National Tour)

 

“16” magazine (February 1972), From Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (article summary/quotes)

This issue of 16 Magazine February 1972 carries an article by two girls who followed the Jackson 5 to Miami and New York with their friends. Sandra Holcomb and Sandra Fludd talk about meeting them at their hotel and having photos taken, before the Jackson 5 did their concert.

Sandra Holcomb attended the 16th July, 1971 at Madison Square Garden, New York. The day before she and her friends met the Jackson 5 at their hotel, once as they were leaving for pizza and later on the way to their show. Both times the girls got the boys autographs, while the second time they approached Michael for photos which he obliged.

“Come on fellas, they want photo’s”.
