Rappin' With the Jackson Five

Date Assessed by the fact that Michael mentions that they had “just did a show in Miami” and they “saw the Temps” the same night. January 2, 1971 was the date that both the J5 and Temps performed in Miami. The interview also took place after January 31, 1971 (They talk about their Gary, Indiana concert)
