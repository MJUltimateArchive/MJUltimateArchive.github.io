20th Grammy Awards Reception

 

Brad Elterman, photographer, personal website

One evening back in 1978,  I was with Michael Jackson at the old Chasen's Restaurant at the corner of Doheny and Beverly Blvd. It was some sort of a Gold Record party and the place was mobbed. Michael had enough of the crowd and we headed out to the sidewalk where one or two fans were hanging around. Michael stood next to the wall, took a deep breath, relaxed and I shot some photos. Chasen's was torn down years ago and today is a Bristol Farms supermarket. You can go there. You can stand on the sidewalk at the exact location where young Michael once stood so tall and handsome.
