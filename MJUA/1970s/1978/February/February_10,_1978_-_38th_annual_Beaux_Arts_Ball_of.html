38th annual Beaux Arts Ball of the National Urban League Guild

Date confirmed in newspapers (“Friday”, February 10, 1978)

 

“Associated Press” (February 9, 1978) (archived)

Stephanie Mills, star of Broadway's "The Wiz," and Michael Jackson of the Jacksons singing group will be honored at the 38th annual Beaux Arts Ball of the National Urban League Guild in the grand ballroom of the Waldorf-Astoria Friday in New York. Jean Young, wife of UN Ambassador Andrew Young, is honorary chairwoman of the "salute to youth" gathering.

 

“Associated Press” (February 9, 1978) (archived)

Singer Michael Jackson and actress Stephanie Mills, star of Broadway's "The Wiz," will be honored in a "salute to youth" at the 38th annual Beaux Arts Ball of the National Urban League Guild Jean Young, wife of U N Ambassador Andrew Young, is honorary chairwoman of the event to be held in the grand ballroom of the Waldorf - Astoria on Friday. Sponsors include musician Lionel Hampton, actress Lynn, Redgrave and local television newscaster Pia Lindstrom, daughter of actress Ingrid Bergman.

 

“Associated Press” (February 10, 1978) (archived)

The 38th annual Beaux Arts Ball of the National Urban League Guild will honor MICHAEL JACKSON and STEPHANIE MILLS, stars of Broadway's “The Wiz,” tonight at New York’s Waldorf-Astoria Hotel. Jean Young, wife of U.N. Ambassador Andrew Young, is honorary chairwoman of the event that includes among its sponsors musician Lionel Hampton. actress Lynn Redgrave and New York TV newscaster Pia Lindstrom, daughter of actress Ingrid Bergman.
