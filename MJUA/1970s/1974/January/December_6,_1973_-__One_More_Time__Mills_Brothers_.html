"One More Time" Mills Brothers Special

Date of filming confirmed by the facts in “Melody Maker” (archived) (“Tomorrow (Friday) they'll tape a show called "One More Time" which [will include] The Jackson Five singing with the likes of Pat Boone and the legendary Mills Brothers.”) The “Melody Maker” interview also took place “one week shy of marriage” for Jermaine (his wedding was on December 15, 1973), so December 6 would be the Friday with more than a week’s time before the wedding. / Air date of January 10, 1974
