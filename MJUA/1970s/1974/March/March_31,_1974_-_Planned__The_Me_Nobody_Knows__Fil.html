Planned "The Me Nobody Knows" Film Starring Role

 

The Galveston Daily News (March 31, 1974)

Michael Jackson of the Jackson Five may have the starring role in producer Gil Cates' film version of "The Me Nobody Knows." A young mimic said he didn't want to be another Rich Little: "I just want to be a little rich".
