"Right On!" Magazine Interview

 

“Right On!” magazine, “Through the Years with Michael Jackson!” (1981)

“Los Angeles is special, like New York. We like to play here when we’re really hot. And we really don’t want to overexpose ourselves here. When we play L.A., it’s really an event.

“Something is really missing when I’m not on stage. I may sound crazy, but I’m a stage addict. When I’m not on stage for a long time, I have fits and I get crazy. I start crying and I act… I guess you might say weird and freaked-out. I start dancing around the house. It’s like part of me is missing and I have this wild craving for it.”
