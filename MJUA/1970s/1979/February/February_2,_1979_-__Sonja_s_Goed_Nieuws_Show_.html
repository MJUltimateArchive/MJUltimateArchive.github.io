"Sonja's Goed Nieuws Show"

Date confirmed in “Amsterdamsdagblad.nl” (archived) (“2 February 1979”)

 

“Amsterdamsdagblad.nl” (April 24, 2017) (English translation)

On 2 February 1979 the time has come: the ultramodern, unique tropical wave pool of De Mirandabad will be opened. For the occasion, Sonja Barend's popular talk show 'Sonja's Good News Show' will be broadcast live from De Mirandabad. Guests are Mayor Polak, artificial swimming group De Meeuwen, Peppi and Kokki. And Michael Jackson.

Lifeguard Martin Teeuw is there as a 22-year-old employee of the De Mirandabad and makes the only preserved images. "I only worked there for a month and did a lot of photography as a hobby. During the shooting of the TV program I quickly took a few photos, without flash. No idea if it was actually allowed."

Martin Teeuw: "It was all very special, but of course work had to be done as well. Michael was already quite famous at the time and was therefore rather protected, but I still had a chat with his brother Jermaine."

The TV images from the opening have been lost forever, both the Vara and the Netherlands Institute for Sound and Vision no longer have the recordings of Sonja's Good News Show of February 2, 1979. That makes Martin Teeuw's photos even more unique. "I still have a few original slides that slowly yellow. I once lent another slide and never got it back."

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

The Jacksons performed on the Sonja's Goed Nieuws Show on 2nd February, 1979. Presented by Sonja Barend The Jacksons performed ‘Shake Your Body (Down To The Ground)’ and ‘Blame It On The Boogie.’ They were at the show for the opening of the new De Mirandabad, a new wave pool.

The Sonja's Goed Nieuws Show was on the Dutch channel VARA.

Michael also meet Dutch comedy duo Kokki & Peppi backstage.
