"Disco in the Snow" / Geneva, Switzerland (Destiny World Tour)

Date of filming confirmed in ABBA on TV program listing (archived) / Aired on April 9, 1979

 

“Rock & Soul” magazine (September 1984)

"One time I got this big package in the mail and all it had on it for a return address was Switzerland”, explained Janet. “I opened up the package and found this big painting set. It had felts and crayons and five big books to go along with it. I mean this package was huge. I didn't know who it was from, because I didn’t know exactly where my brothers were at the lime. Then I decided it had to be from Michael, because both of us like to draw. When they got off the tour, the first thing Michael asked me about was the package. I told him I thought it was from him, but I didn't know for sure since he didn't put his name on it. It was so sweet of him to remember me and I loved the painting set."
