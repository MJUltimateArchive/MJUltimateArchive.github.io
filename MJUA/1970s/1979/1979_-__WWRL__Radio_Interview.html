"WWRL" Radio Interview

Date range confirmed in Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (“1979”)

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Michael and Marlon visited DJ Gary Byrd at WWRL Radio station in New York in 1979, during the Destiny Tour. They were there to promote the tour and their successful album.
