Wings' "Venus and Mars" Album Recording Session Wrap Party

Date confirmed in “PR Newswire” (archived) (“‘Live on the Queen Mary’ documents a legendary performance from the Venus and Mars album release party thrown by Paul and Linda McCartney and Wings in 1975… ‘Live on the Queen Mary’ was recorded March 24, 1975”)

 

Michael Jackson, “Moonwalk” autobiography (1988)

Paul McCartney always tells people this story about me calling him and saying we should write some hit songs together.

But that’s not exactly how we first met.

I saw Paul for the first time at a party on the Queen Mary, which is docked in Long Beach. His daughter Heather got my number from someone and gave me a call to invite me to this big party. She liked our music and we got to talking.

 

Randy Taborelli, The Magic and the Madness

Michael and Tatum first met [in 1975] at a party hosted by Paul McCartney aboard the Queen Mary in Long Beach, California.

 

Cher, singer, “Larry King Live” (June 25, 2009) (archived)

I remember one night we were at a party. I think it was on the Queen Mary. And we danced all night long. And I never even thought about--that I wasn't his equal as a dancer. We were just having a blast.

He was a great teenager. He was a great, optimistic, adorable, not very confident, though. I mean, he was so beautiful and adorable, but he didn't have any confidence in that.

...That night at that party, a guy came over and said something to me about him, and I went, you know what? This is the kind of crap that people start. They just start rumors. You have no idea what you're talking about. Get up from my table; get out of my sight. I don't ever want to talk to you or see you again.

And he said Michael Jackson is going to change the way he looks completely. And he's going to change his skin.

And I went, you know what? I can't tell you what I said. It was all kind of really -- just think of me at my worst, Larry, and you'll know what I said to him.

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Photographer Harry Benson took pictures of Michael that night at the ‘Bon Voyage Party’, which took place 24th March, 1975 in Los Angeles. Having taken along with him brother Randy and his mother Katherine, he was photographed with several showbiz friends that included Dean Martin, Cher and Paul’s late wife Linda.
