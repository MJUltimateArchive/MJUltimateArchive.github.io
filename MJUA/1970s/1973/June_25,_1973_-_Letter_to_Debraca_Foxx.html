 Letter to Debraca Foxx

 

Michael Jackson, letter, Christie’s auction listing (June 26, 2013) (archived)

Hi, we are in Australia; we are packing all the houses. We were flying for two whole days in the air. We were very tired. Randy got very sick. Jackie said “Hello”.

Goodbye

(Annotation) “Ooh Honey” yes
