"Valentine" Magazine Message

Date of publication confirmed in Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

 

Michael Jackson, Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (article summary/quotes)

Michael is asked in this issues article on what groups he likes. Paul McCartney is one particular favourite, along with Sly and the Family Stone. The brothers all have different tastes, Michael said. Tito liked listening to Taj Mahal, while Jermaine liked The Beach Boys and The Supremes.

Michael confirmed that he and brother Jermaine had written songs for Gilbert O’Sullivan and Glen Campbell, but said they had not told them about them. Michael was asked if he had a girlfriend and what her name was:

“Well I only have a pet mouse. I don’t have a girlfriend so I don’t know what her name is yet.”

One fan said Michael looked tired on Top Of The Pops when performing Rockin’ Robin:

“The reason I looked fatigued on that programme was because we had to do the song three times for the cameras. The first two times I was pulled off the stage by fans. By the third time I was more scared than out of breath.”

Asked what sort of girl he’d like to marry Michael’s reply was short and sweet:

“I’m not going to get married.”
