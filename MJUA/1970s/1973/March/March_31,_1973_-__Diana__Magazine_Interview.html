"Diana" Magazine Interview

Date of publication confirmed in Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (“1971”)

 

“Diana” magazine (March 31, 1973), From Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (article summary/quotes)

Each member of the Jackson Five, including Randy, was asked questions individually for this article back in March, 1973.

Amongst the 8 questions Michael was asked were:

What subjects he liked at school. How they got on with the Osmonds and about his idols.

First memory:

“Jackie was riding his bike with Tito on the crossbar and it hit the curb and they went sprawling. I must have been three at the time.”

Subjects he liked at school:

“Maths and physical training.”

The Osmonds:

“We’re great friends, even though being in the same hotel was just a coincidence. I remember I couldn’t sleep one night and I called Donny about 3’o’clock in the morning for a chat.”

His idols:

“Danny Kaye, The Temptations and The Supremes.”
