Tokyo, Japan (Tokyo Music Festival)

 

"Soul" Newspaper (June 25, 1973)

The first concert in Tokyo as part of the Tokyo Broadcasting Association's Tokyo Music Festival.

Very few Blacks were in any of the audiences in Tokyo; two foreign exchange students, both girls, were the only Blacks. They danced in the aisles and carried on as if they were right in the United States. After the show, they met The Jackson Five and were very grateful to have been so lucky to be in Japan this spring.

 

Paul Williams, ASCAP chairman, “Billboard” magazine, from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro

I first met Michael when he was a child, at the Tokyo Music Festival. I’d joked about wanting his tux when he outgrew it.
