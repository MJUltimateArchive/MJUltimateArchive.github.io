"Right On!" and "Teen Raves" Magazines Photoshoot / Interview

 

“Right On!” magazine (July 1973)

Michael, who is the most talkative of the J-5 is also the most popular with the girls--although he doesn't date seriously yet. His two big passions right now are basketball and painting. "I spend all my money on art supplies", Michael says. "I love painting. I do abstract and 'show' art--all those funny designs with lines and things. But I wouldn't sell them. I just give them away to friends and other people. I don't care who sees them."
