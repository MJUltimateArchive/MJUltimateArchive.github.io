"Rock and Soul" Magazine Photoshoot / "Flip" Magazine Interview

Date of “Flip” magazine publication confirmed in Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (“September 1973”)

 

“Flip” magazine (September 1973) (Image 007)

The craziest dream I ever had was kind of a funny nightmare. I’m real scared of fires, so I dream about them a lot. Once I dreamed that a fire started in my house and I smelled smoke and ran into the living room and saw what was happening! Suddenly that part of the fire that was burning up our color TV set started looking like a human torch and it said to me: “Man, you grab your things and git outta here--I’ll give you time to split, man, but go!” I ran like heck!
