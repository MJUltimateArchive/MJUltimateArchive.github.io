THE JACKSON 5 ABC NEWS REPORT

Harry Reasoner

The Jackson 5 featured in a rare news report with Harry Reasoner in 1970. Concert clippings featured: ‘Thank You (Falettinme Be Mice Elf Agin),’ ‘ABC,’ and ‘The Love You Save.’ Footage shows the Jackson 5 arriving at the airport. Some footage from this news report featured on the audio of THE JACKSON 5IVE LIVE AT THE FORUM Double CD.
