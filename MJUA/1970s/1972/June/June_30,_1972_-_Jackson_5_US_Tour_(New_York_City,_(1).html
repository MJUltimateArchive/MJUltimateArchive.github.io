Jackson 5 US Tour (New York City, New York)

 

Michael Jackson, Los Angeles Times (Mirror)

“We had a concert [in New York City] and [Latanya Simmons] (winner of The Dating Game TV show) came and was in the front row and everyone recognized her from television and started grabbing at her [giggles] and wanted her autograph [more giggles]. They had to put her backstage.”

Los Angeles Times (August 30, 1972)  (Mirror)

“I only did one show by myself.” he smiles. “That was the Dating Game. It was fun. I won number one. She got to go to New York to see our concert.”
