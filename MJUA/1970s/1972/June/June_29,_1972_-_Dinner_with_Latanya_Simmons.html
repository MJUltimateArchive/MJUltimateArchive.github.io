Dinner with Latanya Simmons

The host of The Dating Game said they would eat at Sardi’s Restaurant for dinner

 

Michael Jackson, Los Angeles Times (1972)

“We went out to dinner in New York. The next night (June 30, 1972) we had a concert there and she came and was in the front row and everyone recognized her from television and started grabbing at her [giggles] and wanted her autograph [more giggles]. They had to put her backstage.”
