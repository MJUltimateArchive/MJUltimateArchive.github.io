"Tiger Beat Spectacular" Magazine Interview

Date range of publication confirmed in Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (“December 1972”)

 

“Tiger Beat Spectacular” magazine (December 1972), From Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (article summary/quotes)

The Jackson 5 are asked what type of house they would love to live in for this issue of Tiger Beat Spectacular. Michael sees himself living in a big house.

“The biggest room in the house would be the one I’d spend the most time in, completely equipped for all kinds of sound.”

Michael had put a lot of thought into what he’d design which would include: Electronically controlled sliding doors and other gadgets. He said he would also design a room for his collection of items brought over the years.

“Because I like city life, I want the house to be near all the action with a great view of all those twinkling lights at night.”
