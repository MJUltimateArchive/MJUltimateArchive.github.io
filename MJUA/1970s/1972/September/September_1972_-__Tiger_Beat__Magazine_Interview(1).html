"Tiger Beat" Interview

Date confirmed in Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

 

“Tiger Beat” magazine (September 1972), from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (article summary/quotes)

Michael took part in Tiger Beat’s Annual article ‘Dreams They Fear’ in September, 1972’s issue.

Michael’s dream fear centred on a merry go round of horses and zebras. Michael said he dreamed of being thrown off, after a photographer had asked him to be shot alone. Michael said he ended up being thrown against two carved swans, which then wrap their wings around trapping him.

“I somehow know that I will be turning in the carousel for the rest of my life.
