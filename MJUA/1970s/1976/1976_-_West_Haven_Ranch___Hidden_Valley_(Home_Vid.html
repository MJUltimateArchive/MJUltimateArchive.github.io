West Haven Ranch / Hidden Valley (Home Video)

Date assessed from the fact that Barry White purchased the horse Mandingo for his wife Glodean sometime in 1976, (“less than a year before [January 6, 1977]”) and Glodean mentions in the video that “today [she] got on [Mandingo’s] back a little bit and was a little nervous and I got him a little nervous and so we were two nervous things.”, implying that this was her first time riding the horse. Most likely, this video was shot shortly after they purchased Mandingo.

Hidden Valley assessed by the facts that Katherine says “We’re here on Eve Arden’s ranch”, which is confirmed to be “a farm called Hidden Valley” in a 1985 Chicago Tribune article, “1151 Hidden Valley Road in Thousand Oaks” confirmed in Berg Properties listing. I’m assuming the owners (Eve Arden and Brooks West) personally named it “West Haven Ranch” due to several people calling it that in the video.
