Dr. Klein Visit

 

“Daily Star” (January 16, 2009) (archived article scan)

POP oddball Michael Jackson sparks new fears for his health as he makes an exit from a medical centre. Jackson covered his face with a surgical mask, scarf and hat, but fans soon recognized the singer and mobbed him for autographs. He signed and chatted, then munched on a packet of crisps as he was driven away from the Los Angeles Clinic.
