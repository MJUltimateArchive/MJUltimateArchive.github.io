Speaking with Gerry Inzerello

 

Gerry Inzerello, “Independent Online” (IOL), (June 27, 2009) (archived)

I communicated with him last on June 20. I told him I was pitching for the World Cup song. He agreed to write the music to my lyrics if I was awarded the song. Michael was excited and agreed to help me. We were going to meet at his recording studio in LA. It would have been a match made in heaven.
