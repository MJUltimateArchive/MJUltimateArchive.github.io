Ne-Yo Track for Upcoming Album

 

Mirror (July 8, 2008)

Michael Jackson isn't pulling any punches when it comes to his new album - in fact, it sounds like the rhinestone gloves are off.

Singer Ne-Yo, who's penned a track for it, said: "He's being very picky but is supposed to be. This is going to be a really important album."
