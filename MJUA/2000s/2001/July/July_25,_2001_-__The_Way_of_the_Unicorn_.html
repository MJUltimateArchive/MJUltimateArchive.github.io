"The Way of the Unicorn"

Date of report confirmed in “The Guardian” (archived) (“July 25, 2001”)

 

“The Guardian” (July 25, 2001) (archived)

Michael Jackson has agreed to produce the animation film The Way of the Unicorn, The Endangered One for Disney. "This is the new Michael Jackson, the movie actor and producer," Dennis W Peterson of Big International Group of Entertainment told the Associated Press. "This is Michael's future and he wants to devote his energies to it." The 42-year-old will also voice the role of an orphan named Sailor who teams up with a lonely rich girl and a band of endangered animals on a quest to save Mother Earth. It was thought that Jackson's return to the big screen would be in the title role of the big-budget indie flick, The Nightmare of Edgar Allan Poe, his first as a leading man, but the film, announced two years ago, has been stuck in pre-production hell ever since.
