Rabbi Schmuley Boteach and Uri Geller Book Launch

 

ITN (Independent Telelvision News)

Despite his broken foot Michael Jackson has made his first London appearance when he attended the book launch of "Confessions of a Rabbi and a Psychic" by Rabbi Shmuley Boteach and Uri Geller. Adoring fans and photographers gathered in London to catch a glimpse of popstar Michael Jackson when he attended the book launch of "Confessions of a Rabbi and a Psychic" by his friends Rabbi Shmuley Boteach and psychic Uri Geller. Jackson arrived at the Royal Institute of British Architects on Monday evening (March 5) for the book launch, hobbling on crutches after fracturing a bone in his foot at his home in California.
