"Shoes for Charity"

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Michael donated a painted pair of shoes for charity on 9th November, 2003. The idea came from a shoe manufacturer named Tatami who came up with the idea to create a charity titled "Shoes For Charity" for children suffering from traumas. Michael was amongst other celebrities who contributed to the charity.
