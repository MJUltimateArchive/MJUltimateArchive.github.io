"2006 Bahrain Grand Prix" Attendance

Date confirmed on multiple Formula One websites (here and here for example)

 

Tim Cash, MTV News Staff, “MTV News” (July 7, 2009) (archived)

The first time I actually met Michael Jackson was in 2006. I was invited to Bahrain to take part in one of the preliminary car races for the Formula One Grand Prix. Our gracious host was Crown Prince Sheikh Salman bin Hamad bin Isa Al-Khalifa, who was also playing host to another member of royalty, King of Pop Michael Jackson.
