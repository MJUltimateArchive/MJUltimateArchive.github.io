“MTV Video Music Awards Japan” (2006)

Date confirmed in “Associated Press” (archived) (“MTV Video Music Awards Japan 2006 was held in Tokyo on Saturday (27th May [2006])”)

 

Michael Jackson, "MTV Legend Award" acceptance speech transcript

First, I’d like to say thank you MTV Japan… It’s a great honour. And – I love you more. But, most of all, I’d like to say to the Japanese people, I – from the bottom of my heart – I love you very, very much. I promised myself, when I came up here that I wouldn’t get emotional, because I – I think you’re a very sweet, generous, kind people and, from the bottom of my heart, I thank you, I thank the fans around the world and the fans in Japan – I love you more. But I – I thank my children, Prince, Paris and Blanket for being there for me and I love them very much. And I, I say thank you to all the people that believe in me and I believe in you and I love you very much. Thank you so, so much.

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Backstage Michael sent his loyal fans another message through the reporters waiting:

“I love you and thank you for your loyalty.”

A day later he visited the Seibi Gakuen children’s home and watched a 15 minute traditional Japanese performance from the children.

“I’m very happy to be here. I’m happy to be with you lovely people.

In Japanese, he said “Aishiteru,” which translated meant’ I love you.’

Speaking to a reporter later he answered why he had decided to visit Japan:

"Why did I choose to come to Japan in particular? Because I love the culture, I love the people and they're always so gracious and loyal and kind and I just love what they respect, the way they're doing things... Their way of life".
