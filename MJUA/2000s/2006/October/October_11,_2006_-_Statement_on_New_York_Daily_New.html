Statement on New York Daily News Story

 

Raymone Bain, press release

Washington, D.C. …..A photograph in today’s, (October 11, 2006), New York Daily News, allegedly depicting Michael Jackson in women’s shoes and clothing IS NOT Michael Jackson.

Mr. Jackson has not visited Saint-Tropez in recent years, nor has he donned a women’s hat and pumps for disguise. Mr. Jackson is demanding that the New York Daily News offer an apology and retraction, or he will refer the matter to his attorneys for further action.
