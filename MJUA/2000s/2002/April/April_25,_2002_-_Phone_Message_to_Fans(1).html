Phone Message to Fans

Date confirmed in MJNI (archived mirror) (“04/25/02… this morning”)

 

Michael Jackson, speech transcript

Hi, this is Michael Jackson. I just want all my fans around the world to know that I love you from the bottom of my heart. I love you so much. Everything I do is because of you and I want to thank you for supporting me and my career and everything I have done. I wanna thank Planet Jackson, Black And White magazine, MJ News International and Angel. I love you all with all my heart. Thank you for everything you are doing. I love you so much, forever. Thank you. Goodbye.
