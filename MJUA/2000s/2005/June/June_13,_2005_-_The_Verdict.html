The Verdict

 

Tito Jackson, The MJCast Episode 50

At the last court date that he was exonerated from, on the way, I was riding in the car with him and my Mom, and he said, "Tito, I have a present for you when we get to the house."

I said, "Michael, you don't have to give me no present."

He said, "No, I want to give you something"

I said, "No, Mike, I'm ok"

He said, "No, no, I've been thinking about this a long time and I just want to do this for you because I've never done anything for you, and I just want to do this."

So, anyhow, we get to the house, and he takes me to his garage. And inside his garage, he's got like four Bentleys. And he goes, "Pick one."

I said, "What?"

He says, "Pick one."

I said, "Michael, I can't pick one of your cars."

He said, "No, no, no. I have four of these, and I've got a whole let more in the warehouse in Santa Monica.

And I said, "Michael, I can't take one of-"

He said, "Tito, take one of my cars"

So anyhow, I said, "Michael, I don't know which one to pick. They're all beautiful."

He said, "Well, if I was you, I'd pick this one."

And I said, "Why this one?"

He said, "Well, for two reasons. This is the one I drive everyday, and this one, everyone that's sat in this car, I had them sign the ceiling of it."

I said, "Are you sure, Michael?"

He said, "Yeah!"

So he gave me a Bentley, and that was one of the nicest things he's ever done for me... So I have his Bentley with Beyonce's signature in it, and all these other people, so it's a great thing.
