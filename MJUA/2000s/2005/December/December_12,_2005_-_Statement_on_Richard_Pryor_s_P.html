Statement on Richard Pryor's Passing

 

Michael Jackson, Raymone Bain press release (December 12, 2005) (mirror) (archived mirror)

Michael Jackson releases the following statement regarding the death of his friend, Mr. Richard Pryor:

“Richard Pryor was the true ‘King of Comedy’…there will never be another like him. He was a trailblazer, whose extraordinary talent and genius provided the blueprint for others to follow.

“I send my love and heartfelt sympathy to the entire Pryor family.”
