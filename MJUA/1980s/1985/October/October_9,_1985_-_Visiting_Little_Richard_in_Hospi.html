Visiting Little Richard in Hospital

 

Unknown newspaper clipping, Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Michael Jackson kept a secret bedside vigil alongside Little Richard after the veteran rocker's car crash last month. And the megastar made sure the private room at Los Angeles' Cedar Sinai Hospital was always full of fruit and flowers.

Michael, who now owns the publishing rights to Little Richard's greatest hits, is planning to finance a movie version of his wild career.
