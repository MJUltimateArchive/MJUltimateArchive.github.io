Note to Find Guitarist Roger Troutman

Date confirmed on handwritten note (“3/5/85”)

 

Michael Jackson, handwritten note, “Julien’s Live” auction listing (June 23, 2012) (archived)

3/5/85. Please Candy, “Emergency”. Please, you must find the guy who played guitar on the record “Dance Floor” for the band “Zapp”. The album credits will tell you his name. No matter where he is, find him!

M.J.
