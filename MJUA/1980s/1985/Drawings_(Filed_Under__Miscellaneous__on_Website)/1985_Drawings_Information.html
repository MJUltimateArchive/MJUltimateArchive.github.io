1985 Drawings Information

 

Images 001-011, Mirror (November 4, 2018)

The superstar made over 30 drawings of himself and other family members including his sisters LaToya and Janet Jackson.

The intimate artwork dates from 1985 when he was at the peak of his career having released his Thriller album.

He gifted his drawings, which are in black marker pen, to his plastic surgeon, Dr Steven M. Hoefflin.
