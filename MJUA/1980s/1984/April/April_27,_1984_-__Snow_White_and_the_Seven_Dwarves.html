“Snow White and the Seven Dwarves” Encino Visit

Date confirmed in “Associated Press” (archived) (“Friday” [April 27, 1984])

 

“Associated Press” (April 28, 1984) (archived)

Rock superstar Michael Jackson is visited by Snow White and Dopey, one of the seven dwarfs, Friday in Jackson's Encino, Calif, home [and] presented Jackson with a one of a kind display, featuring seven of the popular Disney characters in a representation of the 1937 classic animated film, Snow White and the Seven Dwarves.

“Associated Press” (April 28, 1984) (archived)

Superstar Michael Jackson and Snow White pose with one of the Seven Dwarfs. Jackson received a one-of-a-kind display representing the 1937 classic film "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs" at his Encino, Calif., home.
