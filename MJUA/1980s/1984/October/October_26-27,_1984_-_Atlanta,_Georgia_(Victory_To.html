Atlanta, Georgia (Victory Tour)

 

Unknown magazine article, from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (archived)

UNCF College Presidents Dr Elias Blake Jr (Clark college), Dr. Calvert H. Smith (Morris Brown College), and Dr. Luther S. Williams (Atlanta University) thanked superstar Michael Jackson for donating a portion of his proceeds from the Jacksons’ Victory Tour to the United Negro College Fund. Jackson received UNCF’s 40th Anniversary Humanitarian Award.

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

The Jacksons grandfather Samuel was a specially invited guest for their shows in Atlanta on 27th & 28th October. Speaking just prior to their arrival he spoke fondly of Michael saying he had offered to buy him a house and Rolls Royce.

“I’m quite content and I’m really happy where I am,” he said.
