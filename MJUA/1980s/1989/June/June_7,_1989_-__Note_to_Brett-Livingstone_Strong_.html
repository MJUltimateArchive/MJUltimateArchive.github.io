"Note to Brett-Livingstone Strong"

Date confirmed on the note (Image 000) (“6/7/89”)

 

Michael Jackson, transcript of note

I know the creator will go. But his work survives. That is why to escape death, I attempt to bind my soul to my work.

Michaelangelo

 

To Brett,

 

Love,

Michael Jackson
