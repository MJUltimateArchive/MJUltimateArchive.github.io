Michael Jackson, “The World of Michael Jackson” International Fan Club Newsletter, Newsletter 6

Dear Friends:

All across the United States, Europe, and the Orient these past several months, I have had the true thrill of seeing so many of your smiling faces in the audiences at my concerts. As you know, I had not been on tour for a while, so it is really wonderful to, again, be so overwhelmed by your support and your friendship.

I feel tremendous waves of love coming up out of the audience as soon as I go on stage. And I keep on feeling it after the band’s last notes have long since faded.

Thank you for being such great and loyal fans. It means more to me than you will ever know.

Wishing you peace and great happiness in the coming year.

I love you all!

Michael
