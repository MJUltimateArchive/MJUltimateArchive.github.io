Frank Sinatra and Sammy Davis Jr. Reunion Concert Attendance

Date range confirmed in LA Times (archived) (“Frank Sinatra will headline the Greek Theatre from Aug. 20 to 22 [1987]...who'll be joined on the Greek bill by Sammy Davis Jr.”)

 

"New York Times"(?) (1987)

It was a big night in Hollywood. Frank Sinatra and Sammy Davis wound up their reunion concert and invited a bunch of heavyweight pals to a private, after-the-show party at the Greek Theatre.

All the glitterati of Tinseltown was there. Elizabeth Taylor, Liza Minnelli, Tom Selleck, and Sophia Loren mingled furiously. Alone, in a corner, Loren's date for the evening stood in the shadows, cowering like a cornered animal.

[Sophia Loren's date] was Michael Jackson.
