“Musical Youth” Visit

Date assessed by the fact that their first album was released on October 1982, and the first single for their second album (“Tell Me Why”) was released July 9, 1983 (archived). Most likely recording of their second album started in 1983 and wrapped up around June.

 

Dennis Seaton or Michael Grant (Musical Youth members), “Designer” magazine (April 2003) (archived)

When we were in Los Angeles doing the second album he invited us over to his house, it wasn't Neverland at the time cos he was still at his mum and dads where they all grew up. When we got to the house we were as quiet as mice and he was just really cool and said how he enjoyed our work. After he'd showed us round the house he just let us go off to enjoy ourselves. All the brothers came over, Janet was there, Latoya, Randy, Marlon, Tito, Jermaine, their kids came round, Jane Fonda came and it was nothing but fun.
