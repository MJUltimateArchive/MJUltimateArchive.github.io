US Navy Radio Interview

Date assessed by the fact that they mention that “It’s Stevie Wonder’s 33rd birthday [May 13, 1983] this week, so Rex asked Michael Jackson to tell us a little about the birthday boy”. This most likely occurred earlier that month before this interview aired.

Stevie Wonder is, you know... God I've learnt so much from him by just sitting in on his sessions and talking to him and listening to... I mean, he's phenomenal.
