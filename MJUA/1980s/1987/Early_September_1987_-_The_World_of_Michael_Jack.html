“The World of Michael Jackson” Newsletter 4

Date range of written message assessed by the fact that it is implied the “Bad” tour was close, but had not yet started, and the fact that it was published in Fall 1987 (MJJCollectors item listing)

 

Michael Jackson, “The World of Michael Jackson” International Fan Club Newsletter, Newsletter 4

Dear Friends:

By the time you read this special edition of my “World”, our international “Bad” tour will be off and rocking! We’re all elated to, again, be making live music for all of you, all over the world. I hope that many of you will be able to see our show. It’s full of excitement and lots of surprises. I hope you like it!

As you listen to my new album, I ask you to pay serious attention to one special line in the lyrics of “Man in the Mirror”, a beautiful new song by Siedah Garrett and Glen Ballard that means so much to me.

“If you want to make the world a better place, take a look at yourself, and make a change.” Think about it.

My heartfelt thank you for being my friend. I love you all!

Michael
