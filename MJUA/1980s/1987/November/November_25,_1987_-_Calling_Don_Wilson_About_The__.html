Calling Don Wilson About The "Man in the Mirror" Music Video / Short Film

Date confirmed by Don Wilson (“day after Thanksgiving 1987”)

 

Don Wilson, Magazine Americana (June 2009)

I got a call to meet with Michael the day after Thanksgiving 1987 to talk about doing a video for the song, so, needless to say, it was a magical thing for me. It was an honor to be chosen to do the video and a task I did not take lightly.

Rolling Stone (June 24, 2014)

Larry [Stossel, Epic exec] told me Michael wanted to do something real heart-wrenching and tell a story, and could I go meet with him. This was the day after Thanksgiving.
