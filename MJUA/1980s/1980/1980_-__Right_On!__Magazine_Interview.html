"Right On!" Magazine Interview

 

“Right On!” magazine, “Through the Years with Michael Jackson!” (1981)

“I’m not particularly into clothes.

“I don’t like to break hearts. Some girls have been into me for so long – buying records, pasting pictures on their walls, going to concerts. They wake up seeing me, and go to sleep seeing me. When they meet me in person, they have been knowing me for a long time, but I don’t know them. That’s the painful part of show business. The breaking of hearts.

“I believe in the Bible. I know that I’m an imperfect person. I’m not an angel and I’m not a devil either. I try to be as best as I can and I try to do what I think is right.”
