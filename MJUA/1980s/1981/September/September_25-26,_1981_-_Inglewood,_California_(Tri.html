Inglewood, California (Triumph Tour)

 

Bill Wolfer, keyboardist, “Invincible” magazine (November 2014) (mirror) (archived mirror)

The tour ended with three nights at the Los Angeles Forum, and the end-of-tour party on the last night was packed with celebrities, including Quincy Jones, Steven Spielberg, Stevie Wonder, Magic Johnson, Prince and others.
