West Berlin, Germany (Bad World Tour)

 

Bob Jones, publicist, "Black Radio Exclusive" magazine (July 22, 1988)

West Berlin—June 18. As widely reported in the American media, we played The Wall, which symbolically and literally divides this historic city. A wildly enthusiastic crowd estimated between 45-50,000 watched Michael's performance, while the East Germans staged a 'competing' show hosted by Olympic figure skating Gold Medalist Caterina Witt and headlined by Bryan Adams. But that didn't stop 3,000 highly vocal Michael Jackson fans across The Wall in East Berlin from protesting their government's refusal to let them see their idol. We were moved, saddened and eternally grateful to be citizens of a democratic society.
