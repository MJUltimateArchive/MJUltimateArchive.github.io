Rotterdam, Netherlands (Bad World Tour)

 

Bob Jones, publicist, "Black Radio Exclusive" magazine (July 22, 1988)

Rotterdam—June 3. Our three shows at Feyenoord Stadium attracted 45,000 each night. Here we saw a Black population in evidence for the first time. They originally came from the former South American colony Dutch Guiana, now called Suriname. Don't draw any conclusions, but we encountered our first Kentucky Fried Chicken here, too!
