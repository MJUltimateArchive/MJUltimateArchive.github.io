Video Message to Mandy Porter

Date range confirmed in Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro” (“In 1996”); this will be updated with more sources if more information comes to light.

 

Michael Jackson, video message transcript

Mandy. Mandy Porter. I hope you're feeling better. I miss you and love you and tell all your friends who worked on the dance performance, that was brilliant. Tell them I loved it very much and enjoyed it very much. May you have the best of joy and peace and good health in the future and forever. Love always, M.J.
