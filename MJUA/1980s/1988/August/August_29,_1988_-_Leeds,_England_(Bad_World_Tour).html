Leeds, England (Bad World Tour)

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Before the show Michael donated £90,000 to the “Give For Life” charity-organization. Proceeds from the concert were contributed towards helping immunize 40,000 children.

90,000 fans at the Leeds concert on 29th August, before Another Part Of Me, sang Happy Birthday to Michael on his 30th birthday.
