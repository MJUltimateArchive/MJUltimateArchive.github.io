“The World of Michael Jackson” Newsletter 5

Date range of publication confirmed in MJJCollectors item listing

 

Michael Jackson, “The World of Michael Jackson” International Fan Club Newsletter, Newsletter 5 (sourced from transcript in Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Dear Friends:

It’s really great to have a chance to say hello again! By now, many of you have already been in the audience for our big “Bad” world tour. I can’t tell you how wonderful it is to be back performing, live and in person, for all of my friends all over the world. The fant~stic reception we have received so far has been a “Thriller”!

As the tour continues, I hope so much that every single one of you will be with me for one of my shows! And thanks also for your enthusiasm and your loyal support to the “Bad” album. You are great. I love you!

Michael
