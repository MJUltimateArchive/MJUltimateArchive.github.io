“Earth Song”

Date range / date confirmed in Mike Smallcombe’s “Making Michael” (“Besides some additional vocals, Michael completed a version of ‘Earth Song’ in 1990 with Bill Bottrell… Bottrell said his version was completed by August 1990, and that the Canadian only changed the outro vocals at the end from falsetto to full voice, and added the guitar riff in the second verse [for the final version in “HIStory”]... The hectic final [“HIStory”] sessions took place over the last weekend of March [1995]... The final night of recording was late on the Saturday [(March 25]) night into Sunday morning, and this was when Michael sang the huge ad-libs at the end of ‘Earth Song’.
