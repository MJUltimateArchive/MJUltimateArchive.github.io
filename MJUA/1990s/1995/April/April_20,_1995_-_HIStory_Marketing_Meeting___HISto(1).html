Shana Mangatal, Facebook post

Michael Jackson gave this [script/meeting notes document (Image 002)] to me on April 20, 1995. He often taped his acceptance speeches and other pre- taped announcements for other countries, at the office where I worked (his manager). This is his personal script for thanking his fans in France, Holland and Germany. MJ wrote at the bottom "NOW". On the back of this (Image 001) is also an original handwritten note. He handed this to me when he was finished with his meetings for the day.
