"Childhood" Music Video / Short Film

Date range confirmed in François Allard and Richard Lecocq’s “Michael Jackson: All the Songs: The Story Behind Every Track” (“Shoot date: 30 May to 2 June 1995”)

Promo short film directed by Nick Brandt, shot in a fantasy forest. Filmed in Sequoia National Park, with ships filled with children (including the young star of the Free Willy films, Jason James Richter) floating over the treetops – paid homage to Peter Pan.
