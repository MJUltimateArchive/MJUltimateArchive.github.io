"Scream" Music Video / Short Film

Date range sourced from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson Maestro” (“Scream took 12 days to film and began shooting 15th May, 1995”). This fits the sources we do have: First day of shooting confirmed in this fax to David Bradshaw, styler for Michael and Janet, May 1995 also confirmed in “Making of” documentary [top left corner at 0:07] a “15” day timecode and “19” day timecode at 3:37 and 15:39 respectively, and [what looks like] a “5/16” on the clapperboard on this “Making Of” segment at 1:25; this date range also fits Janet’s statement that “It was only supposed to be a... 3-day shoot, and it turned into like a week” [Will update this date range if more information comes to light]
