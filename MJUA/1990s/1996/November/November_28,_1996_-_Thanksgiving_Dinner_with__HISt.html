Thanksgiving Dinner with "HIStory World Tour" Crew

 

Freddie Washington, “HIStory World Tour” bass player, “L.A. Watts Times” (June 24, 2010) (archived)

The fondest memory I have of Michael Jackson was when we were on the 1996 HIStory Tour in Australia, we all had Thanksgiving dinner together and my 5-year-old son went up to him and took a photo of Michael. He thought it was so funny and just cracked up. [I appreciate] his bigger-than-life heart.
