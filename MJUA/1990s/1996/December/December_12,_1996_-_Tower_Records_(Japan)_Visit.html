Tower Records (Japan) Visit

Date confirmed in Adrian Grant’s “Visual Documentary”

 

Michael Jackson, speech transcript, from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Hello, thank you very much for having me. I just want you to know I love you all very much and I’m glad to be back in Japan. Thank you. You, guys sound beautiful singing harmony. I just want to say thank you again for having me here and I’m very happy to be back in Japan and I love you more. I love you more. Thank you.

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

While in Japan for his History tour on 12th December, 1996 Michael attended a ceremony at Tower Records to set his hands in plaster for a plaque to be permanently displayed in the shop.
