VH1 Interview

Date range assessed by the fact that it takes place in “Thailand 1996” (confirmed on MTV News and on video) and it could not have been the day he arrived on November 5, 1996, because, due to rain and “feeling a little under the weather… the day of the show (November 5), Michael stayed in his hotel” (Inside Edition). November 8th was the last possible day since he had a show November 9th in Auckland.
