Seoul, South Korea (HIStory World Tour)

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

For the Seoul concert at Chamsil Olympic Stadium guitarist Jennifer Batten was unable to play because of illness, in her place was Greg Howe.

In Bombay a fashion company tried to get the show stopped, calling for his arrest because of his cancellation three years earlier. Meanwhile Mahesh Thackeray threatened to burn himself in the stadium because the Shiva Sena State government refused him the venue to raise money for AIDS awareness programmes.

After being greeted by an ecstatic crowd at the airport and having a garland wrapped around his neck Michael said:

“I would just like to say that I am happy to be in India to support the charity. I am looking forward to the concert.”
