Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia (HIStory World Tour)

 

Christie Eliezer, music journalist, “HIStory Preview” (October 27, 1996 show)

Royalty from four local provinces attended Jackson's recent Kuala Lumpur show. Local tradition has it that nobility remains at such functions for only 45 minutes. However, at the Sunday show, three princes stayed until the end of the concert.
