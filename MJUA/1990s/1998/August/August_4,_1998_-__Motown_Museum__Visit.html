"Motown Museum" Visit

Date confirmed in “Reuters” photo description (“August 4 [1998]”)

 

“Reuters” photo description (accessed January 24, 2020) (archived)

Music stars Michael Jackson and Stevie Wonder along with Esther Gordy Edward, sister of Berry Gordy, founder of Motown, visit the Motown Museum August 4 in Detroit. [Both Jackson and Wonder are in Detroit to support businessman Don H. Barden's run for a Detroit casino license on the August 4 primary election. Both stars started their careers at Motown].
