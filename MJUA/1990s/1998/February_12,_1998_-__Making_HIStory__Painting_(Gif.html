"Making HIStory" Painting (Gift from author Adrian Grant)

Date confirmed in Adrian Grant’s “Making HIStory” book (“I presented this wonderful painting (right) to him on the 12th February, 1998.”)

 

Adrian Grant, author, “Making HIStory” book

To celebrate my ten year association with Michael Jackson, I presented this wonderful painting to him on the 12th February, 1998. Needless to say Michael was truly delighted with the tribute, which was created by British born artist Vincent McKoy. The original oil painting is entitled “Making HIStory” and now hangs proudly in Michael’s home.
