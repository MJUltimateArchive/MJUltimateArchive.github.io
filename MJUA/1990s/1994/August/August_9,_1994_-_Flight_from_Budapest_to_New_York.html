Flight from Budapest to New York

Newlyweds Michael Jackson and Lisa Marie Presley held hands at Heathrow yesterday.

...The couple made a brief show of affection as they jetted in from Hungary, where Jackson, 35, filmed a video.

One worker at their Budapest hotel told how the singer plastered powders and potions on his face in the mornings.

He said the star--who arrived with only two small suitcases, but brought his own cooks--appeared "unpretentious". He added: "He seemed very polite."

But the worker, who spent an hour in the couple's suite, claimed he ever heard Jackson speak with his 26-year-old bride.

Jackson and Lisa Marie flew to London on his private jet. They stayed only 30 minutes before heading out again on Concorde to New York.
