Letter to "Bravo" Magazine

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

In 1993/94 Bravo magazine held a letter campaign supporting Michael against the accusation of child molestation. Bravo received over 50,000 responses in ten days. Michael sent a letter to Bravo magazines readers to thank them for all of their support and gifts. Dated 6th January, 1994 the letter read:.

“Thank you for the love and support you recently showed me by sending many wonderful letters and gifts.

I sincerely appreciate your undying love, compassion, concern and support during these times. It means a lot to me.

I have been strengthened in my fight to prove my innocence by my faith in God and by the knowledge that I am not fighting this battle alone. Together we will see this through.

I love you all,

Always.

Michael Jackson
