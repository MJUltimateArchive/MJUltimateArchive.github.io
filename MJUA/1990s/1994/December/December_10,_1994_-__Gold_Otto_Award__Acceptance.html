"Gold Otto Award" Acceptance

 

Michael Jackson, speech transcript

Hello, Dortmund! I’m sorry I can’t be with you tonight. But I’d like to thank all of you for the Golden OTTO Award. And I hope to see you all soon. I appreciate everything you have done, and your never-ending love and support. Thank you so much. I love you. Bye.
