Ryan White's Visitation Attendance

 

IndyStar (April 9, 2015)

Tuesday, thousands attended the visitation at the funeral home, including John, who arrived in a Pontiac Trans Sport van. Jackson and his crew showed up in a Ford sedan. Only Lukas Haas came Hollywood-style, Roberts recalls. Haas, who was 13, played Ryan in the TV biopic. He rode in the back of a stretch limousine.
