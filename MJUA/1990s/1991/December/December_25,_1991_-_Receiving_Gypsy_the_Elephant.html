Receiving Gypsy the Elephant

Date confirmed in Elephant database entry for Gypsy (“1991-12-25”); year also confirmed in “Michael Jackson's Private Home Movies” (“1991” overlayed over footage)

 

Elizabeth Taylor, “Elizabeth Taylor: My Love Affair with Jewelry” 2002 autobiography

Soon after Larry Fortensky and I got married at Michael’s Neverland Valley Ranch, I couldn’t think of what to give Michael to show my undying thanks. He rarely invites anyone to Neverland, and this was the first time, which was so generous, such a glamorous compliment to our friendship. I had been trying to think of a truly meaningful way to thank him. Then I got an idea: Michael has a zoo. I’ll get him an elephant! That clinched it. I got him a great big Asian elephant named Gypsy. I guess you could say we exchanged elephants.
