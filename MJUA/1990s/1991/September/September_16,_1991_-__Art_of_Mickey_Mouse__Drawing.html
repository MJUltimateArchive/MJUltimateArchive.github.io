"Art of Mickey Mouse" Drawing

Date of “New York” magazine publication confirmed on magazine cover / Date of November 30, 1991 publication for “Art of Mickey Mouse” publication confirmed on Google Books and “New York” magazine (“Nov 30, 1991” and “published in November [1991]”, respectively)

 

“New York” magazine (September 16, 1991) (archived)

An unlikely artist has contributed a sketch for an upcoming book on Mickey Mouse: Michael Jackson. Appearing alongside artwork by Keith Haring, Andy Warhol, Maurice Sendak, and Charles Schulz is an ink drawing of the famed cartoon character--done for the project by Jackson. "He's a giant Mickey fan", says Craig Yoe, a co-author of "The Art of Mickey Mouse", to be published in November by Disney's Hyperion. "[Jackson] carried a sketchbook around until the right moment of inspiration hit him."
