London "Dangerous" Album Listening Party

 

"The Juice" (November 1991)

More than 150,000 was splashed out on a lavish party to launch Michael Jackson's new "Dangerous" LP last week, and, of course, "The Juice" was there.

...400 engraved gold invitations were [illegible] for the official album playback at London's extremely plush Savoy Hotel, held one day before the record was due in the shops. The entire banqueting room had been extravagantly decorated with images from the album sleeve and, while the album was played, [illegible] and a shortened version of the "Black or White" vid were simultaneously projected on screens around the room.

The highlight of the evening was a personal filmed message from Michael himself, who regretted not being able to attend the party in person, but said how much he loved everyone in Britain anyway!

After the playback, guests, including DJs Gary Davies, Pat Sharp, and Simon Bates, were several champagne from special Michael Jackson bottles. Other Jacksonabalia include After Eight mints in Jackson wrappers!

 

Melody Maker (1991)
