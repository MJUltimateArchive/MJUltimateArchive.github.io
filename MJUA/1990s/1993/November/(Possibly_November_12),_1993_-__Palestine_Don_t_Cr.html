"Palestine Don't Cry"

Date estimated by the facts that the year is confirmed as 1993,and the song was written on British Airways Concorde stationery. This particular date was Michael’s trip to London. Could also be from Michael’s trip from London, will update this as more information comes to light.

 

“Julien’s Auctions” item listing (June 24, 2010) (archived)

Two-pages, one with writing on both sides, of working lyrics written by Michael Jackson on British Airways Concorde stationary in black pen. 8 1/4 by 5 13/16 inches (1993)
