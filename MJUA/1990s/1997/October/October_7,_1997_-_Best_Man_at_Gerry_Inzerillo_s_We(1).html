Best Man at Gerry Inzerillo's Wedding to Prudence Solomon

Date confirmed on AP Archive (“[Michael] was a special guest at a wedding in Cape Town on Tuesday [October 7, 1997].”)

 

AP Archive (Tape 1)

Popular music star Michael Jackson is taking South Africa by storm on his first tour of the country.

He is performing to capacity crowds and winning hearts -- including that of President Nelson Mandela.

Mandela, his companion Graca Machel and her three children were surprise guests at Jackson's concert in Cape Town on Monday night.

Jackson has also taken the chance to look up old friends - he was a special guest at a wedding in Cape Town on Tuesday.

The King of Pop was best man at the wedding of South African T-V personality Prudence Solomon and Gerry Inzerello, who heads the Ian Schrager Group of Hotels in the U-S.

Inzerello is on tour with the star.

SOUNDBITE: (English)

"Michael and I have been friends for 23 years. I don't see him as a superstar performer as much as I see him as a member of the family and someone that we love very much. So it is very nice to have him here on this special day."

SUPER CAPTION: Gerry Inzerello, Groom

The wedding was held at the residence of United States Ambassador, James Joseph, who also conducted the ceremony.

Solomon, who now lives in New York City, recently started work as an anchor for N-B-C Cable Television.

His new wife was obviously delighted with their even more famous guest.

SOUNDBITE: (English)

"Well it's great, I mean he's such a great friend and we feel honoured that he's here, he's been wonderful."

SUPER CAPTION: Prudence Solomon, Bride

Jackson played to capacity crowds in Cape Town on Saturday and Monday nights. More than 70-thousand people attended the shows.

Jackson will perform twice in Johannesburg and once in Durban before jetting off to Paris.

 

AP Archive (Tape 2)

Taking time out from his concert tour of South Africa, Jackson was a special guest of honour at a high society wedding where well known local TV personality Prudence Solomon married hotel magnet Gerry Inzerillo. The simple ceremony at the Cape Town home of US ambassador James Joseph attracted several luminaries including former Miss South Africa Amy Kleynhans and film producer Anant Singh. But all eyes were on Jackson when he escorted a glowing Solomon down the aisle.
