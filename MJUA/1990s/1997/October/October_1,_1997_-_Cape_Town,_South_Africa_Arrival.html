Cape Town, South Africa Arrival

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Arriving for the final leg of his tour in South Africa he was greeted by around 500 fans at Cape Town airport. He was presented with a gift of a shirt from an African tribe, the Cape Minstrels.

Just prior to arriving in Cape Town, a message Michael had made was aired on TV.

“Hi, this is Michael Jackson. Hello South Africa. I look forward to seeing you in October. I love you.”
