“Uncut” magazine (September 1997) (RocksBackPages) (archived scan, sides cut)

*Grab transcript from RocksBackPages*

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Michael opened his UK gigs at the Dom Valley Stadium in Sheffield on 9th July, 1997. Before the show he handed over the keys for six coaches that would be distributed around schools in Sheffield. Speaking at the press conference, accompanied by his wife Debbie Rowe, Michael said:

“I would like to say a few words. Children will always form a special place in my heart, and I would like to present on behalf of Heal The World and myself, these gifts. And there is much more where this came from.”
