Crystal Lee King's Funeral Attendance

Date assessed from the fact that his grandmother Crystal Lee King passed away on November 4, 1992, so the funeral would have happened around this date.

 

Unknown magazine snippet

Michael Jackson made a rare public appearance with his family when he traveled to Phoenix, Arizona for his grandmother's funeral. But even in grief, the rift of the family continued. Although he was seated next to his father, Joe, for the service for Crystal Jackson at the Jehovah's Witness church she attended every Sunday, Michael did not speak to them. And he ignored his brothers, his former members in the Jackson Five.
