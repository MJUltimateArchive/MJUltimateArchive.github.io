Istanbul, Turkey Arrival

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Despite landing at Ataturk airport in Turkey for his first ever show in a Muslim country, two days later before he’s due to arrive at the venue, the performance is called off. After discovering he had nodules on his vocal cords by a Harley Street specialist in London the European tour comes to an end.
