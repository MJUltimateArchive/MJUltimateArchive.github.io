"Leaganul de Copii Sfanta Ecaterin" Orphanage Visit

Date confirmed in “UPI” (archived) (“Wednesday [September 30, 1992]”)

 

“UPI” (October 1, 1992) (archived)

Jacksonmania is sweeping Romania! Thousands of Romanians turned out to try to catch a glimpse of Michael Jackson, in Bucharest to give a concert Thursday night as part of his European tour. The pop superstar joined Romanian President Ion Iliescu Wednesday at the opening of a playground for orphans. Jackson was late for the appearance and the president was left cooling his heels for nearly an hour until he showed. The playground was financed by Jackson's Heal the World Charity. 'We must heal each other and ourselves...and celebrate the joy of giving,' Jackson told an audience of Romanian VIPs and Western journalists. He is the biggest star to visit the nation since the bloody revolution three years ago and even Premier Theodor Stolojan took time off from his busy schedule to greet him. 'This is an extraordinary event for Romania. You have brought a ray of light to the country,' the premier said, although he seemed somewhat amused by the singer's costume — a military-style black jacket, black jeans, black hat, sunglasses, and plenty of makeup.

 

“The Observer” (October 3, 1992) (archived) (mirror with full transcript) (archived)

Prime Minister Teodor Stolojan, who has kept Romania on an even keel for the past year, was another VIP. At a press conference he paid tribute to Jackson's "extraordinary talent" and spoke for many Romanians when he said he hoped the world would now begin to "see the dignity and hope of Romania in this difficult time."

He sat in patient perplexity while Jackson addressed the conference for just a minute and a half, saying: "The life blood of ages is dancing in our bodies at this moment. Let's join in love and unity and consciousness and make a sacred pledge to heal each other and thus ourselves."

...[Jackson] has launched a new charity "Heal the World," which aims at harmonizing the planet by attending to the needs of children.

...Jackson and President Iliescu toured the country's most comfortable orphanage, to which Jackson has donated a playground.

 

Hannah Crossdale, daughter of “Playdale Playgrounds” owner (April 2003) (archived)

In 1992, my Dad, John Croasdale, received a phone call from a man who wanted Playdale to build a playground for a big orphanage in Bucharest, Romania, for 500 children. That was the furthest Playdale had ever been to build a playground, the man did not say who wanted the playground to be built, so the designers and installers got to work, they built the playground in the factories and sent it in a massive lorry to Romania, six people went along too, when they arrived in Bucharest, they discovered that MICHAEL JACKSON was the mystery client, and he wanted the playground for his Michael Jackson ‘Heal the World’ foundation. Michael Jackson opened the playground and stuck his thumbs up at the crowd to say thank you.

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

[Michael Jackson] visited the Leaganul De Copii Sfanta Ecaterin to open a children’s playground his Heal The World foundation as funded.

On 30th September Michael held a press conference at the “Teatrul de Revista Constantin Tanase” theatre in Bucharest Romania. In the presence of Prime Minister, Theodor Stolojan he addressed the press and attendee’s.

"I’m very happy to be here... in Romania, and (?) my concert live on HBO, and to help the children. Dear friends, ladies and gentlemen, Mr. Prime Minister. These children are as dear to me as all the children of the world. Today, I’ve had the opportunity to see the face of transformation and civilization. A moment like this means healing the world. We must heal the world and ourselves. We have to weave our life like a web, because we are part of this web. Out of negligence, we have contaminated and polluted our planet and, in exchange, we hurt ourselves. In the life songs of ages is the dance of our blood. The environment is an extension of our body and it gives the pulse of love, is the orchestra of universe’s symphony. It is music’s territory and enables us to rediscover our souls. Let’s rejoice together in love today, unity and conciliation. Let’s heal everybody and, thus, heal ourselves. Let us make a covenant with life and let us celebrate with joy a new world... and healed. Thank you... especially, to all the children of the world. I love you all. Thank you so much.. Thank you.."

Following this he was treated to a version of his ‘Heal The World’ song from a children’s choir.

...Michael made time for some private time at the Snagov Monastery, that was built by Vlad Tepes. ("Tepes” meaning “the Impaler” or as some history documents show Vlad Dracula).

...A playground at an orphanage, and a medical mission to Romania by 12 qualified doctors/medical staff, were among the first projects the [Heal the World] Foundation funded.
