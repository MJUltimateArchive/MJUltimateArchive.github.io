"Gone Too Soon" Music Video / Short Film

July-August 1992 date range of first version of “Gone Too Soon” music video airing (containing funeral footage) confirmed in “Chicago Sun-Times” (July 3, 1992) (“The video, to be released this summer [1992]”) / December 1, 1993 date of second version of music video airing, along with the “Gone Too Soon” single, confirmed in “The Atlantic” (archived) (“[“Gone Too Soon”] was released as a single on World AIDS Day, December 1, 1993.”)

 

“Chicago Sun-Times” (July 3, 1992) (archived)

Jeanne White says a Michael Jackson video to the song "Gone Too Soon" will show how much the pop star cared for her son, Ryan White.

The video, to be released this summer, will include images of Ryan from home movies and photo albums.
