"50 Million Award" Acceptance

 

"The Sun" tabloid newspaper

Michael Jackson [is throwing] a spectacular party to celebrate the launch of his new world tour tonight.

The 33-year-old singer has splashed out on a host of attractions for the late-night bash--including a huge funfair, three dance floors, and a dazzling fireworks display.

More than 1,000 guests will join the party...

Cabaret

They will be treated to a luxury buffet and cabaret-style floor shows from dancers flown in from America for the event.

Jackson will be guest of honor and will receive a Sony award for the biggest-selling pop artist of all time.

He is also visiting hospitals in every city on the tour to donate thousands of pounds to sick children.
