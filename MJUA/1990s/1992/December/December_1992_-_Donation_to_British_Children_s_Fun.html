Donation to British Children's Fundraiser for Bosnian Victims

Date range assessed by the fact that Michael’s statement in the newspaper article was “from Japan”; he was in Japan for his “Dangerous World Tour” in December 1992.

 

Unknown newspaper article (1992), from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Pop superstar Michael Jackson has praised the efforts of British children for raising £20,000 to help starving victims of the Bosnian crisis. The 1,200 youngsters from the North West were £5,000 short of their target until Jackson stepped in with a gift from his Heal the World charity to make up the total. Speaking from Japan yesterday, he said: “I wish the entire world would give as much as these children are giving.”
