December 12-31, 1992 - Tokyo, Japan (Dangerous World Tour)

 

Michael Jackson, from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Just prior to his arrival in Japan Michael sent the following message:

“I’m Michael Jackson Chairman of Heal The World Foundation. This is a special message for all of you listening to the Pon Broadcasting System and Musicthon. I will be performing at the Tokyo Dome between December 12th to the 31st. I wanna hear what you perceive to be the biggest problems in our world and the best solutions on how to solve them. Please join me in healing the world and making it a better place to live in. I’m looking forward to meeting the winners at my concert at the Tokyo Dome. I love you all very much.”

 

December 30, 1992 Concert:

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

At 30th and 31st December concerts at Japan’s Tokyo Dome, Michael was joined on stage by Slash, for Black or White.

 

December 31, 1992 Concert:

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

At 30th and 31st December concerts at Japan’s Tokyo Dome, Michael was joined on stage by Slash, for Black or White.

Michael wished everyone a Happy New Year from his dressing room backstage before the show.

That night the band played ‘Auld Lang Syne’ after ‘Heal The World’.
