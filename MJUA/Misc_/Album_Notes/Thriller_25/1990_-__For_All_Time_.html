Mike Smallcombe, “Making Michael”

Contrary to popular belief, “For All Time” wasn’t recorded for Thriller. The song was actually written by “Human Nature” writer Steve Porcaro and his writing partner Michael Sherwood for Dangerous, and a demo was recorded at Record One in 1990. “Michael recorded it but it wasn’t quite finished,” Porcaro said.
