"Different Kind of Lady"

Date range for “Goin’ Places” tracks confirmed in François Allard and Richard Lecocq’s “Michael Jackson: All the Songs: The Story Behind Every Track” (“December 1976 - August 1977”)

 

Michael Jackson, “Valley News” (August 18, 1977), from Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

Michael raved about ‘Different Kind Of Lady’ to Valley News on 18th August, 1977.

“In our new album I like ‘Different Kind Of Lady’. It’s smoking. You can dance to it.”

“Billboard” magazine (January 12, 1980)

I think what really convinced people." says Jackson, "was when we wrote a song on the ‘Goin' Places’ album, 'Different Kind Of Lady,' which was a big hit in the clubs. Even Kenny Gamble came and told us, ‘You all are good enough to do your own stuff.'"
