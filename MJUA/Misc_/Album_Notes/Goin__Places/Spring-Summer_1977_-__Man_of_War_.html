"Man of War"

Date range confirmed in Kit O’Toole’s “Michael Jackson FAQ”

 

Kenny Gamble, album producer and co-writer of song, The Philadelphia Tribune (August 28, 2012 (archived)

“Man of War” was one of [Michael’s] favorite songs [out of the ones we wrote], 'cause our whole thing was messages. “Message in the music,” and that's what Michael wanted to do. He said, “Man, you know? I want to do some messages.”
