"One More Chance" Music Video / Short Film

 

Nick Brandt, director, Black or White Magazine interview (Google translation of German translation)

Black or White Magazine: What was the concept of the clip?

Nick Brandt: The video was played in a performance hall, but the roles were changed: the audience was on the stage under the headlights, and Michael was in the auditorium, where the audiences are usually. So he should sing to convince the audience to give him "one more chance". He had to use his whole heart, imploring him as he bounces from one table to the other and dances.

Black or White Magazine: How far were you when the Arvizo affair broke loose and the film was canceled?

Brandt: The shooting lasted only one day. On the evening of the first day of shooting, Michael was arrested by the police. I never talked to him again ...

Black or White Magazine: What had you done?

Brandt: We had all the pictures of Michael turned from behind as he sings to the audience, which is on the stage. The next day we wanted to film him from the front.
