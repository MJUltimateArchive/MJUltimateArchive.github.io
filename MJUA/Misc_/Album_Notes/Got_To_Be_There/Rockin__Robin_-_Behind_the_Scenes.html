Rockin' Robin - Behind the Scenes

 

Michael Jackson, Right On! Magazine

“I knew Rockin’ Robin’ was going to happen. I knew it was going to be really good.”

 

Tom Bahler, vocal arranger, Articlesbase (archived)

Tom said he can't remember every song he worked on with Jackson while at Motown, but said he has beautiful memories, filled with "funny moments." "There were so many songs, that if I hear them, then I can remember them," he said. Still, one song stands out in his mind: Jackson's version of "Rockin' Robin." Tom remembers helping him practice his vocals for the song.
