"The Lost Children"

 

Michael Jackson, News of the World Interview (November 4, 2001)

News of the World: Tell me about the message in your song The Lost Children.

Michael Jackson: I'm saying there are souls out there that are lost. People who have disappeared and have never been found again. I remember when I was a little kid, I was in a department store with my mother. I was no more than five, I think. I turned around and she was gone.

I'll never forget the feeling. I felt my world was ending. So imagine really, really being lost. It's like Armageddon of the brain.
