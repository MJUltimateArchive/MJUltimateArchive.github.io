Untitled Song 1

 

“Julien’s Auctions” (December 3, 2011) (archived)

Two pages of Michael Jackson handwritten working lyrics for an unrecorded song that begins "Party People the youth we gotta teach..." The pages have many of the same lyrics but are written with different writing implements and have different annotations on each. 11 by 8 1/2 inches.
