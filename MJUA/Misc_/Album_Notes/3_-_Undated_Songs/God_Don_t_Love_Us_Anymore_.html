"God Don't Love Us Anymore"

 

“Julien’s Live” auction listing (June 24, 2010) (archived)

A single page of paper with provocative handwritten lyrics written in black ink in Jackson's hand. They read in part, …"Well they say that their [sic] the native people, tried to win or fight a losing war. They say that the white man knows what he's fighting for." There is a small section of writing at the bottom of the page reading, "note = as children stand there crying seeing their mothers or = asking what are we fighting for." 8 1/2 by 11 inches
