“Julien’s Live” auction listing ( October 8, 2010) (archived)

Written on the back of a "Song Packet Info" sheet, this song was written by Jackson in 1993 at the height of his sexual abuse controversy.  The lyrics read in part, "If they won't take me in Cairo then lord where will I go.  I'll die a man without a country and only god knew I was innocent now.
