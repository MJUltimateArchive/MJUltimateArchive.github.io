“Work That Body”

Date range confirmed in Kit O’Toole’s “Michael Jackson FAQ: All That's Left to Know About the King of Pop”

 

Bryan Loren, percussion/guitar/collaborator, “Myspace” blog (July 11, 2009) (archived)

‘Work That Body’, is complete with Michael repeating(at my behest) the rap from The Jackson Five’s ‘ABC’. He DID NOT want to do this(lol), but realized the tongue-in-cheek fun contained in it. I produced this track, and am performing all of the music. There are no sequencers on it, as I do not generally use them in the course of my work. The two of us wrote the lyrics and melody.
