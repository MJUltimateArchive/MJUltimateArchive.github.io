Jerry Marcellino, producer, “StarFacTree” press release (June 26, 2009) (archived)

The amazing thing about his talent in those days, he would learn a song by hearing it three or four times and he would know the song and record it perfectly. One example of the great singer he was naturally, was when we recorded the standard classic “All the Things You Are” by Rodgers and Hammerstein, which is difficult to sing in the first place with so many chord changes and vocal range. He would hear it, run through it a couple of times, and sing it perfectly.
