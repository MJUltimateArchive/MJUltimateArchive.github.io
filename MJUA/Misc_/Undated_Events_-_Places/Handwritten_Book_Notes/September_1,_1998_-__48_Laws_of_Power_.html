Date of book’s release confirmed on “Barnes & Noble” book listing (archived) (“Pub. Date: 09/01/1998”)

 

Michael Jackson, various handwritten annotations, “Bonhams” auction listing (June 29, 2011) (archived)

Make yourself respected, a god [Ed. Note: For context, he is annotating a section he underlined where noble courtier Diane de Poitiers identified herself with a Roman goddess to give “her an air of respectability” with the courts.]. Demand Worship… No more talking silence is more powerful, and you create your own circumstances even in the manner in which you are treated and looked upon, and deer are special because they hide if they walked the streets like dogs no one would care the moon comes every night so people don’t care to look to the heavens Haley’s Comet, the fact it comes once in a lifetime makes it important.

“Omega Auctions” Lot 277, listing (November 27, 2018) (archived)

48 LAWS OF POWER ANNOTATED BY MICHAEL JACKSON. A copy of Robert Greene's - The 48 Laws of Power (Viking, 1998) annotated throughout in various inks by Michael Jackson. Choice comments include "You create your own circumstances even in the manner in which (sic) you are treated and looked upon". Sold originally at Bonhams Entertainment Memorabilia sale of 29 June 2011, lot 262.
