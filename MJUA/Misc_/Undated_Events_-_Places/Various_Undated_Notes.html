Julien’s Live listing (June 25, 2011) (archived)

A single page of paper with typed lyrics to a song titled "Carnival (Of Celebration)." The reverse blank side of the page has a note written in Michael Jackson's hand in green ink reading in full, "By the talent given me by God Traning (crossed out) Training cultivating it. Studying the greats in my field and becoming greater. like a scientist searching persistent confident to be the Best. Study the greats and become greater. Reach the Zenith of my ability as an actor singer dancer. M.J."
