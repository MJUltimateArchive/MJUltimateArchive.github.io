Neil Sedaka

 

Neil Sedaka, singer/songwriter, Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

In an interview on BBC 1 on 8th July, 2009, songwriter and performer Neil Sedaka looked back on meeting Michael. He said every time they met up Michael always told him ‘Laughter In The Rain’ was one of his favourite songs. Ironically, Sedaka performed the song on American Bandstand in January, 1975, on the same show Michael performed, ‘We’re Almost There.’
