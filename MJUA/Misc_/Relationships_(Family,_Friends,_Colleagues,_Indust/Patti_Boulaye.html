Patti Boulaye, singer/actress/artist, Telegraph (published by Mick Brown)

...the first time that I’d met Michael. When he was 17, the Jackson 5 came to London to perform at Wembley. I was singing with a girl group called The Flirtations. I was introduced  to him at a club called Gulliver’s. He was a very shy, pimply young man.

We talked, and I just fell in love with his spirit. When he smiled there was so much innocence in it. It really touched your heart.
