Ronnie Rancifer ("The Jackson 5" Keyboardist Until 1975)

 

Ronnie Rancifer, “The Jackson 5" keyboardist until 1975, “Vocalo” radio interview (June 30, 2009) (archived), speech transcript

Ronnie Rancifer: Michael as a child, he was a mischievous child. He liked to play pranks.

"Vocalo": What’s a Michael Jackson prank?

Rancifer: A good Michael Jackson prank is like when you’re asleep in the bed and he’d sneak in there and hit you with a water balloon while you were asleep or something. Pillow fights, he was notorious for pillow fights. One real funny one was coming home one night for a party or something, and he had this boa constrictor thing…

"Vocalo": Wait a minute, wait a minute. That sounds terrible, that sounds like the scariest thing I’d ever heard…

Rancifer: Michael loves animals.

"Vocalo": Where’d he get a…

Rancifer: Jermaine and Mike had this boa constrictor named Crusher.

"Vocalo": What was he called?

Rancifer: Crusher.

"Vocalo": Well, there you go.

Rancifer: He’d be playing with him and believe me he would wrap around your arm and you could feel him constricting you.

"Vocalo": He’d put a hamster in there for Crusher…

Rancifer: He put him in the bed… I said, “Johnny [Jackson], get your butt out here.”

"Vocalo": This is your bed Ronnie that it’s in?

Rancifer: Yeah.

"Vocalo": My question is what happens if you lay down, I mean who knows what you’re doing before then, you’ve had a few too many and you lay down and try to go to sleep?

Rancifer: Well, the bottom line is always learn to check your bed, like the cowboys say, always check your boots in the morning before you put them on, ’cause there might be a scorpion in there, so you always check.

"Vocalo": Alright so always check your bed before you sleep near The Jackson 5.
