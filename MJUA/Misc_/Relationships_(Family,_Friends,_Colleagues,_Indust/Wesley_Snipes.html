Wesley Snipes

 

Power 105.1 (summary/quote)

Speaking with Ed Lover & Malikha Mallette of NYC’s Power 105.1 Snipes told how he once met Michael in his hotel room in South Africa. On the floor in the room were a collection of different books around him that he was reading and studying from:

“I once spent three hours speaking with Michael about metaphysics, psychology, ‘how the black man is treated. People don’t know about Mike on the real, Mike had a consciousness that could blow your mind and he could recite things that could blow your mind as well.”
