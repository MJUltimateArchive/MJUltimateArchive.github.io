Jermaine Jackson

 

Jermaine Jackson, “Right On! Focus” magazine (1984)

Michael and I got into a debate about Gandhi and E.T. that shows you the difference between us. He's into the magical world and loved E.T. But I felt that Gandhi would last longer as a classic because the public could feel that it was about something real. It's the difference between "Dancing Machine" and "Never Can Say Goodbye" will be around long after "Dancing Machine" is gone because it's about real feelings.
