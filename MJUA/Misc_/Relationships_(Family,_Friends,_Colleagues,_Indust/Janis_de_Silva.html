Janis de Silva

 

“Q” magazine (August 1995)

Q: What made Michael Jackson the world's most popular musical star of the '80s and early '90s, maybe of all time?

Janis de Silva: I met his personal magician a couple of weeks ago--his name is Majestic Magnificent--and he was telling me that Michael has this aura about him. It's magical actually...

Q: Hang on a minute. What does this personal magician do for him?

Janis: He puts on private shows at the home and things like that, for the family when they have parties. He's known Michael for seven years.
