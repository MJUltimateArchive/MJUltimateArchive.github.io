Nick Brandt, director, Black or White Magazine interview (Google translation of German translation)

Black or White: Few directors had the opportunity to work with Michael Jackson so often. What are your memories of your cooperation?

Nick Brandt: Working with Michael was initially a tremendous joy because he gave me a lot of freedom. He also gave me all his confidence. Working with him could also be very difficult for two reasons. Just because of this lighting history. It made me crazy that he insisted on always being over-exposed. And secondly, he always came late for the shoot, so I had not got enough time to shoot what was planned and it always ended up tearing my documents and improvising.
